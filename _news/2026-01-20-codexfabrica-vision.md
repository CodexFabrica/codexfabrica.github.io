At CodexFabrica, our vision is simple yet profound: **We build tools that make complex tasks feel natural.**

As a division of **Everything Engineering**, we are dedicated to creating software that empowers the next generation of researchers and creators. We believe that the tools you use should never be a barrier to your creativity or productivity.

### Our Philosophy
1. **Accessibility**: Modern tools should be easy to use regardless of technical background.
2. **Open Source**: Transparency and community collaboration drive innovation.
3. **Refined Design**: We believe in the marriage of high-level functionality and premium aesthetics.

Whether it is our flagship workspace, Feder, or our upcoming research initiatives, every project at CodexFabrica is a reflection of these core values. We are not just building software; we are building a better way to work.

Stay tuned as we continue to expand our ecosystem and push the boundaries of what open-source tools can achieve.
