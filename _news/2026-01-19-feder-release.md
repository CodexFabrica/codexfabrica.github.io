We are proud to announce the official release of **Feder**, an open-source Markdown-based workspace designed to empower writers, researchers, and developers.

Feder was born out of the need for a more intuitive and lightweight alternative to complex typesetting systems like LaTeX and Overleaf. By leveraging the simplicity of Markdown combined with a professional workspace environment, Feder allows you to focus on what matters most: your content.

### Key Features of Feder:
- **Intuitive Interface**: A clean, distraction-free environment tailored for long-form writing.
- **Markdown-First Workflow**: Full support for GitHub Flavored Markdown (GFM).
- **Integrated Previews**: Real-time rendering of your documents.
- **Open Source**: Built with community collaboration in mind.
